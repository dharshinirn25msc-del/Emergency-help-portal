Safety Instructions

Simple and important steps to follow during different emergency situations.

Flood Safety

  • Move to higher ground immediately.
  • Do not walk or drive through flooded roads.
  • Keep drinking water and emergency medicines ready.
  • Switch off electrical supply if water enters the home.

Fire Safety

  • Use the nearest exit and avoid lifts.
  • Call fire service and stay low if there is smoke.
  • Do not open hot doors quickly.
  • Cover nose and mouth with a cloth while escaping.

Earthquake Safety

  • Drop, cover, and hold under a strong table.
  • Stay away from glass, windows, and heavy shelves.
  • Move to open space after shaking stops.
  • Check for injuries and call emergency support if needed.

Storm / Cyclone Safety

  • Stay indoors and away from weak structures.
  • Keep torch, food, and battery backup ready.
  • Follow official alerts and avoid travel.
  • Move to nearby shelter centers if instructed.
