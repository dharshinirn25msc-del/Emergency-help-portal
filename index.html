Main Features

This project is designed to solve a real-world problem by collecting important emergency information into one simple, professional website.

01

Emergency Contacts

Displays ambulance, police, fire service, district helpline, and hospital numbers for quick access.

02

Relief Centers

Shows nearby shelter points with location, contact details, and available facilities.

03

Safety Instructions

Provides easy steps to follow during disasters like flood, fire, earthquake, and storms.

04

Help Request

Users can submit requests for rescue, food, shelter, or medical assistance through a form.

05

Volunteer Registration

Supporters can register to offer help such as transport, food supply, rescue, or first aid.

06

Awareness Platform

The site spreads preparedness awareness and helps people respond calmly during critical situations.

Why This Portal Matters

During emergencies, people often panic and do not know where to get help. This portal reduces confusion and saves time.
